Ringworld posted on Tue, Nov 1, 2011

by Larry Niven

Tagged as: reviews  scifi 

No wonder this has won both Hugo and Nebula awards in 1970/71.

I was not very fond of Niven’s writing style (this is the first Larry Niven book I read) and maybe he used it for this book only. The plot though was ingenious. The idea of the Ringworld was very interesting. It kept me entertained from the beginning until the end.

Highly recommended! :-) Read more about the plot on wikipedia.
