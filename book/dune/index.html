Dune

by Frank Herbert

Tagged as: review  scifi 

Jan 6 2011
  • ?

I was looking to print a nice PnP (Print and Play) game and I came across Dune.

The game is nicely redone, from it’s original 1979 publishing, so thick with theme from the book, that really made me wanna read the book again. First time I read it was while a was a freshman in university (back in ‘89).

And in case you didn’t know, this book is often quoted as “THE best science fiction novel” with more than 12 million copies sold worldwide.

The wikipedia article is very informative, too.
